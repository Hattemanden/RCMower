## Connections
Connect NRF and Arduino as follows:

Signal  | RF Module Pin | Arduino
:------:|:-------------:|:------:
GND     | 1             | GND
VCC     | 2             | 3V3
CE      | 3             | 9
CSN     | 4             | 10
SCK     | 5             | 13
MOSI    | 6             | 11
MISO    | 7             | 12
IRQ     | 8             | -
